# Privacy Policy

Your privacy and personal information is very important to us.
We will not disclose any of your personal information to anyone unless it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defends its legal rights.

We understand the importance of personal privacy. GDPR requests can be requested through our email contact.

Your personal information will never be used externally except for shipping/payments and we will never sell your personal private information.

We also use personal information to:

- Communicate with you;
- Screen our orders for potential risk or fraud; and
- When in line with the preferences you have shared with us, provide you with information or advertising relating to our products or services.
- Cookies

If you leave a comment/review on our site you may opt-in to saving your name, email address and website in cookies. These are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again when you leave another comment. These cookies will last for one year.

If you visit our login page, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

When you log in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for a few days, and screen options cookies last for longer. If you select “Remember Me”, your login will persist for two weeks. If you log out of your account, the login cookies will be removed.

## Data We Collect

### Email Addresses

We won’t trade, rent or sell email addresses to other parties. We do collect email addresses that are given to us through our optional mailing list request forms and from online orders. If you’ve given us your email address, we may contact you with news about new products, services or upcoming events and promotions. If you choose to opt out of these mailings, you may do so at any time. Email addresses may be used to temporarily send shipping updates with a relevant tracking provider when making an order.

### Postal Addresses

We do not trade, rent or sell postal addresses to any other parties. Postal addresses are only collected to ship orders and retained for contacting individuals with a question or information regarding their order.

### Telephone Numbers

We don’t trade, rent or sell telephone numbers to anyone. Telephone numbers are only used for contacting people with questions or information about their orders. Phone numbers may be used to temporarily send shipping updates with a relevant tracking provider when making an order.

### Aggregate Site Statistics

We collect, in aggregate form only, the domain names of visitors to our site, as well as statistics about our sales, traffic patterns and related site information. These statistics include no personally identifying information and are used for marketing, sales reporting or improving the design of our website.
